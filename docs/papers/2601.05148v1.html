Summary

This paper introduces Atlas 2, Atlas 2-B, and Atlas 2-S, a new family of pathology vision foundation models designed to overcome existing tradeoffs in performance, robustness, and computational requirements that previously limited clinical deployment. Trained on the largest pathology foundation model dataset to date (5.5 million histopathology whole slide images), these models achieved state-of-the-art performance across eighty public benchmarks, paving the way for practical clinical integration.
