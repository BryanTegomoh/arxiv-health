Summary

ChatDRex is a novel conversational, multi-agent AI system designed to democratize network-based drug repurposing prediction by providing natural language access to complex bioinformatics analyses. It leverages the integrated NeDRex knowledge graph and specialized agents, enabling physicians and researchers without computer science expertise to identify disease modules and propose drug candidates. This system aims to accelerate the discovery of novel therapies and advance personalized medicine through efficient in silico predictions.

Medical Relevance

This innovation is crucial for accelerating drug discovery by offering a time-efficient and cost-effective alternative to traditional drug development through repurposing. It empowers clinical experts to directly generate hypotheses and explore novel therapeutic strategies, thereby advancing personalized medicine and translational research.

AI Health Application

  • Addresses critical challenges in in silico drug repurposing, including fragmented tools, heterogeneous data landscapes, and the need for effective collaboration across diverse specializations.
  • Introduces ChatDRex as a conversation-based, multi-agent system specifically engineered for network-based drug repurposing prediction.

  • Features specialized bioinformatics agents for network analysis, drug repurposing algorithm execution, functional coherence evaluation (for in silico validation), literature mining, and scientific discussion of results.
  • Employs a flexible multi-agent design that intelligently routes queries, retrieves data, executes algorithms, and visualizes results.
  • Incorporates a dedicated reasoning module to maintain user involvement, provide transparency, and detect potential AI hallucinations, enhancing reliability.

Methodology

ChatDRex employs a conversational multi-agent AI architecture integrated with the NeDRex systems medicine knowledge graph. It utilizes natural language processing to interpret user queries, routing them to specialized bioinformatics agents. These agents execute tasks such as network analysis, drug repurposing algorithms, functional coherence evaluation, and literature mining. The system also includes a reasoning module for user interaction and hallucination detection.

Key Findings

The paper presents ChatDRex as a functional and robust conversational, multi-agent system capable of facilitating complex bioinformatic analyses for network-based drug repurposing prediction. It demonstrates that natural language access to extensive biomedical knowledge and integrated specialized agents can effectively bridge the gap between clinical experts and advanced computational tools, enabling hypothesis generation and candidate identification with enhanced transparency via its reasoning module.

Abstract

Repurposing approved drugs offers a time-efficient and cost-effective alternative to traditional drug development. However, in silico prediction of repurposing candidates is challenging and requires the effective collaboration of specialists in various fields, including pharmacology, medicine, biology, and bioinformatics. Fragmented, specialized algorithms and tools often address only narrow aspects of the overall problem, and heterogeneous, unstructured data landscapes require specialized users to be involved. Hence, these data services do not integrate smoothly across workflows. With ChatDRex, we present a conversation-based, multi-agent system that facilitates the execution of complex bioinformatic analyses aiming for network-based drug repurposing prediction. It builds on the integrated systems medicine knowledge graph NeDRex. ChatDRex provides natural language access to its extensive biomedical KG and integrates bioinformatics agents for network analysis and drug repurposing, complemented by agents for functional coherence evaluation for in silico validation, as well as agents for literature mining and for discussing the obtained results in a scientific context. Its flexible multi-agent design assigns specific tasks to specialized agents, including query routing, data retrieval, algorithm execution, and result visualization. A dedicated reasoning module keeps the user in the loop and allows for hallucination detection. By enabling physicians and researchers without computer science expertise to control complex analyses in natural language, ChatDRex democratizes access to bioinformatics as an important resource for drug repurposing. It enables clinical experts to generate hypotheses and explore drug repurposing opportunities, ultimately accelerating the discovery of novel therapies and advancing personalized medicine and translational research.
